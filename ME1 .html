My Neighbor Totoro


My Neighbor Totoro is a 1988 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki and animated by Studio Ghibli. The story is about two girls named Satsuki and Mei who moved to a new home due to their sick mother. The house they moved in seemed normal at first but little did they know that there would be mystical cratures that will lead them to see the world in a different light

Howl's Moving Castle

Howl's Moving Castle is a 2004 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli. It is a story of a girl named Sophie who was cursed to transform into an old lady by a witch. Along her journey, she meets a man named Howl who lives in a strange moving castle. She comes along with him as they try to find a cure not just for her but the other curses she discovers along her way.
